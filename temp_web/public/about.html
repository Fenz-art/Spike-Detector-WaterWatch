Our Mission

To protect communities worldwide by leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence and data analytics to detect, predict, and prevent waterborne disease outbreaks before they become public health crises.

Protecting Public Health Through Innovation

Waterborne diseases remain one of the most significant public health challenges globally, affecting millions of people each year. Early detection is critical to preventing widespread outbreaks.

Our platform was born from the recognition that traditional disease surveillance methods often lag behind real-world events. By the time health authorities recognize an outbreak pattern, it may have already spread through communities.

We developed an AI-powered solution that analyzes medical records in real-time, detecting anomalous spikes in waterborne disease cases with unprecedented accuracy. Our system uses advanced statistical methods including Z-score analysis and moving averages to identify outbreaks at their earliest stages, enabling rapid response and potentially saving thousands of lives.
